Nonfiction publishing services

Serious nonfiction needs a system, not a stack of vendors.

Sentinel House Press coordinates editorial, design, production, distribution, and author-platform work for mission-driven experts, social-impact professionals, consultants, speakers, and nonfiction writers.

Discuss your nonfiction project

Turn expertise into a reader experience.

A strong nonfiction book is not a transcript of everything the author knows. It needs a defined reader, a disciplined argument or narrative, a useful structure, clear evidence, a coherent voice, and a reason to exist beyond professional credentialing.

Integrated support

Services may include manuscript assessment, developmental editing, copyediting, proofreading, cover and interior design, print and ebook production, metadata, KDP and IngramSpark setup, author website design, launch planning, and measurement. Each part should support the same positioning.

Professional guidance without surrendering control

Authors retain copyright, publishing accounts, royalties, and final decisions unless a separate written agreement explicitly states otherwise. Sentinel House Press provides judgment, coordination, technical execution, and quality control.

Who this is for

The work is best suited to authors with a substantial or complete manuscript, a clear purpose, realistic expectations, and willingness to revise. It is not designed for guaranteed sales, instant publication, or unlimited custom work under an undefined package.

Start with the actual decision

The first question is not which package to buy. It is what the manuscript needs, which publishing route fits the goals, what risks exist, and which investment is justified. A free inquiry identifies the likely route. A paid manuscript assessment or publishing consultation creates deeper clarity before production begins.
