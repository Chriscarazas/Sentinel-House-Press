What is the manuscript trying to become?

ASSESS → SHAPE → PRODUCE → POSITIONAPPROVED

Seven approval gates

The smallest machine that can keep its promises.

Each gate resolves a different type of risk. Nothing advances merely because everyone is tired of discussing it.

01 / ASSESS

Project assessment

Genre, stage, word count, goals, audience, timeline, budget, rights, and known complications.

02 / SCOPE

Recommendation and scope

Deliverables, responsibilities, exclusions, revision limits, pricing, payment schedule, and approval points.

03 / SHAPE

Editorial development

The manuscript is strengthened at the structural, developmental, sentence, or proof level it actually needs.

04 / MAKE

Design and production

Cover, interior, print files, ebook files, metadata, retailer copy, and controlled review cycles.

05 / VERIFY

Quality control

Proofs, files, links, settings, metadata, specifications, and account ownership are checked before release.

06 / POSITION

Platform and launch

Website, sales path, launch sequence, reader communication, distribution, and measurement plan.

07 / HANDOFF

Closeout

Organized files, credentials, documentation, ownership, support boundaries, and next steps.
