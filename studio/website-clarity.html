Flagship engagement

Website Clarity Sprint.

Turn an overloaded or unclear informational website into a focused visitor journey with a stronger message, cleaner structure, and implementation-ready copy.

Best for

Organizations whose websites make visitors work too hard.

Common symptoms include navigation built around internal departments, overlapping program pages, vague homepage claims, missing proof, and calls to action that appear only after a determined expedition.

Typical timeline: 3–6 weeks

Typical review rounds: 2

Final formats: copy deck, sitemap, implementation brief

Optional: simple-site implementation

Audit and diagnosis

  • Current-site review
  • Audience and task analysis
  • Content duplication and gap review
  • Analytics review where available

Structure and message

  • Revised sitemap and navigation
  • Homepage positioning
  • Message hierarchy
  • Calls to action and conversion paths

Content and handoff

  • Homepage and selected core-page copy
  • Page-by-page content brief
  • Visual hierarchy recommendations
  • Implementation-ready copy deck

What changes

Before: the website describes the organization.
After: it helps the visitor understand and act.

The revised site answers, in order: What is this? Who is it for? Why does it matter? What proof exists? What should I do next?

Client responsibilities

  • Provide current content and brand materials
  • Share analytics where available
  • Identify priority audiences and actions
  • Provide factual review
  • Appoint one accountable approver

Outside core scope

  • Large custom software builds
  • Complex ecommerce
  • Full visual identity replacement
  • Daily content management
  • Unlimited stakeholder interviews
  • Ongoing SEO retainers
