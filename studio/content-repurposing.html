One Source, Many Stories.

Turn one substantial report, proposal, interview, webinar, presentation, or research product into a coordinated set of communications for different audiences.

Best for

Organizations with valuable material already created.

The source exists. The expertise exists. What is missing is the editorial work required to extract the strongest ideas, adapt them for real readers, and publish them in a useful sequence.

Typical timeline: 3–5 weeks

Typical review rounds: 2

Anchor output: one substantial piece

Adaptations: defined before kickoff

  • Review existing material
  • Identify reusable evidence and stories

Audience map

  • Define priority readers
  • Match channels to purpose
  • Choose tone, length, and action
  • Sequence publication

Content production

  • Develop one anchor piece
  • Create agreed adaptations
  • Maintain message consistency
  • Deliver editable channel-ready copy
