Selective supporting engagement

Content Development Sprint

For organizations with expertise, research, program experience, or leadership insight that has not yet been shaped into publishable content.

A sprint may include
  • One strategy interview
  • Theme identification
  • Content outline
  • One substantial article, case study, or feature
  • Editing and refinement
  • Shorter adaptations for newsletters, websites, or professional platforms

How engagements are scoped

Project-based. Bounded. Complete.

Every proposal defines the audience, source material, deliverables, timeline, client responsibilities, revision rounds, exclusions, file formats, payment schedule, and final handoff.

Not included by default
  • Daily social-media management
  • Unlimited graphic design
  • Crisis communications
  • Large custom software builds
  • Open-ended meetings without a decision owner
