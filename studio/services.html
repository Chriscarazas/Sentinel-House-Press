Flagship engagement 01

The Stronger Report

For donor reports, annual reports, impact reports, research publications, evaluation findings, and other substantial documents.

You may have

  • A long draft assembled by several contributors
  • Findings buried beneath background
  • An executive summary that summarizes but does not prioritize
  • Charts without a clear message
  • Inconsistent tone and terminology

We do

  • Editorial and structural assessment
  • Report architecture and section sequence
  • Rewriting and line editing
  • Executive-summary development
  • Visual hierarchy, chart guidance, and layout

You receive

  • Approved report structure
  • Revised and proofread narrative
  • Decision-ready executive summary
  • Editable final document
  • Polished, publication-ready PDF

Typical engagement

4 to 10 weeks. Usually two structured review rounds. New research, legal review, translation, and unlimited stakeholder revisions are separate.

Flagship engagement 02

Website Clarity Sprint

For organizations with overloaded navigation, vague positioning, duplicated pages, or a homepage that cannot explain the work.

You may have

  • Too many pages and overlapping labels
  • Program language written for insiders
  • No clear visitor journey
  • Calls to action buried or inconsistent
  • Important proof spread across the site

We do

  • Content and message audit
  • Audience-path analysis
  • Revised sitemap and navigation
  • Homepage positioning
  • Core-page copy and calls to action

You receive

  • Message hierarchy
  • Revised sitemap
  • Homepage and selected core-page copy
  • Page-by-page content brief
  • Implementation recommendations

Typical engagement

3 to 6 weeks. Optional implementation is available for focused informational sites. Complex software, ecommerce, and full brand redesign are separate.

Flagship engagement 03

One Source, Many Stories

For organizations that have already invested in a report, proposal, interview, webinar, or research product and need it to work harder.

You may have

  • A major report used only at launch
  • Research that is too technical for public channels
  • Leadership insight trapped in a webinar
  • Strong stories without a publishing plan
  • Teams repeatedly starting from blank pages

We do

  • Source assessment
  • Audience and channel map
  • Message extraction
  • Anchor-piece development
  • Channel-specific adaptation

You receive

  • One substantial anchor piece
  • A defined set of shorter adaptations
  • Suggested publishing sequence
  • Reusable message bank
  • Editable copy for selected channels

Typical engagement

3 to 5 weeks. The exact number of outputs is agreed before work begins. Daily social media and endless adaptation are not quietly smuggled into scope.
