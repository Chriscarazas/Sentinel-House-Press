What the work includes, and where it stops.

Donor, impact, annual, research, evaluation, and substantial public-facing reports.

Core deliverables

A clear report architecture, stronger executive summary, revised narrative, visible findings, polished layout, proofread editable file, and final PDF.

Typical timeline

4 to 10 weeks, depending on source-material condition, length, design complexity, and approval speed.
