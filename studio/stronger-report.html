Flagship engagement

The Stronger Report.

Turn a dense donor, annual, impact, research, or evaluation report into a document that makes the main finding visible, the evidence credible, and the next decision clear.

Best for

Substantial reports with strong material and a weak reading path.

Common symptoms include repeated background, competing voices, long sections without clear findings, charts that report rather than explain, and an executive summary that never quite reaches the decision.

Typical timeline: 4–10 weeks

Typical review rounds: 2

Final formats: editable source file and polished PDF

Client lead: one accountable approver

Editorial architecture

  • Source review and report diagnosis
  • Revised outline and section order
  • Finding and recommendation hierarchy
  • Identification of duplication and gaps

Writing and synthesis

  • Executive-summary development
  • Structural editing and rewriting
  • Headings, transitions, and callouts
  • Technical-to-public translation

Presentation and finish

  • Visual hierarchy and page layout
  • Chart and table treatment
  • Proofreading
  • Editable file and final PDF

What changes

Before: the report contains the answer.
After: the reader can find it.

The final document gives each audience a route through context, evidence, interpretation, limitations, and action without forcing everyone to read every page in the same way.

Client responsibilities

  • Provide source files and brand guidance
  • Identify factual reviewers
  • Consolidate stakeholder feedback
  • Return reviews within agreed windows
  • Approve structure before full development

Outside core scope

  • New primary research
  • Legal or regulatory review
  • Unlimited interviews or revisions
  • Translation
  • Printing and distribution
  • Ongoing communications management
