A strong fit

  • You have substantive source material.
  • You know who must understand it.
  • You can identify one accountable approver.
  • You are open to structure and editorial challenge.
  • You can meet review deadlines.

A poor fit

  • You need unlimited design for a fixed fee.
  • You want daily social-media management.
  • The project has no decision owner.
  • Every stakeholder can reverse approved work.
  • The deadline exists mainly because planning did not.

A smaller team by design

You may not need a large agency.

You may need one experienced person who can understand the material, identify the message, strengthen the writing, improve the presentation, and bring the project to completion.
