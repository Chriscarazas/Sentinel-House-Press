SECTION 4.3 / PROGRAM PERFORMANCE

Implementation remained broadly consistent across participating sites, with several adaptations introduced during the reporting period.

Partner feedback indicated a need for additional support at transition points and during periods of lower engagement.

Participants who received ongoing coaching were 38% more likely to complete the program.

Further analysis is recommended to understand variation across locations and participant groups.

Why the Studio exists

The same editorial conviction. A different kind of source material.

Sentinel House Press began with books. Sentinel House Studio grew from the same underlying belief: important work should not be buried beneath weak structure, unclear language, or unfinished presentation.

The Studio draws on experience across social-impact measurement, international development, donor and public reporting, research synthesis, executive communication, writing, visual presentation, and digital platforms.

The promise is informed judgment and disciplined completion, not an agency-sized performance of meetings about meetings.
