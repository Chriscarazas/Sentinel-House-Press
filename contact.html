A useful first note

Give us enough to understand the book.

Your message does not need to be polished. Use the prompts below so we can understand the manuscript, the practical constraints, and the support you may need.

  • Name
  • Email
  • Genre
  • Manuscript stage
  • Approximate word count
  • Likely budget range
  • What are you trying to publish, and why now?
  • What support do you believe you need?
  • Target timeline and fixed dates
Please do not attach a full manuscript yet. If the project appears aligned, secure file-transfer instructions will follow.
