Accessibility

Sentinel House Press aims to provide a website that is usable by as many people as reasonably possible.

Our approach

The site uses semantic HTML, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, text alternatives for meaningful images, responsive layouts, readable contrast, and reduced-motion support.

Known limitations

Some decorative editorial artwork may be complex, but essential information should remain available in text. Third-party services connected later, including form or analytics providers, may have separate accessibility behavior.

Feedback

Once the company email address is finalized, this page should include a direct accessibility contact channel. Reports should describe the page, issue, browser, and assistive technology where possible.

Continuous improvement

Accessibility will be reviewed as the site, services, and content evolve.
