Privacy

A practical privacy starting point.

This page is a launch draft and should be reviewed against the final analytics, form, email, payment, and hosting tools used by Sentinel House Press.

Important: Have this notice reviewed by qualified counsel before launch. Privacy law remains stubbornly unconvinced by elegant typography.

Information collected

The website may collect information that visitors voluntarily submit through inquiry forms, email subscriptions, purchases, or direct communication. This may include names, email addresses, project details, manuscript information, and other information supplied by the visitor.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, evaluate project fit, provide contracted services, manage client relationships, improve the website, measure website performance, and meet legal or administrative obligations.

Analytics and third parties

The final notice should identify the analytics, form-processing, email, payment, scheduling, and hosting providers actually used. Do not list tools that have not been installed.

Data choices

Visitors should be given a clear method to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information where applicable. Add the correct company email before launch.

Updates

Update this notice whenever the website’s data practices, vendors, or legal requirements change.
